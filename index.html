About Key Limes

Key Limes are rare and valuable! You can earn them randomly when producing regular limes.
Tip: The more limes you produce, the more chances you have to find Key Limes!

Upgrades

Special Upgrades

Special Upgrades are powerful enhancements that can only be purchased with Key Limes.
Available Upgrades:

  • Lime Boost (5 Key Limes): Doubles your lime production speed.
  • Lime Luck (10 Key Limes): Increases your chance of finding Key Limes.
  • Lime Mastery (20 Key Limes): Unlocks new features and upgrades.

Prestige

Prestige Level: 0

Prestige Bonus: 1x

Prestige allows you to reset your progress in exchange for permanent bonuses.
How it works:

  • You can prestige once you have at least 10,000 limes.
  • When you prestige, you lose all limes and upgrades but gain a prestige level.
  • Each prestige level increases your lime production speed permanently.
